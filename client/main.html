OptiBurn Exercise Optimisation Project

This project involves building an optimisation model using Julia's JuMP and Gurobi/Cplex packages to optimise and derive a set of activities within a specific time limit according to user's input preference for types of exercises and the user's BMI, mainly targeting users who have busy lifestyles but want to lose weight.

The Coca Cola Business Analytics Project

Data-mining through Coca Cola's social media channels such as Facebook, Twitter, Youtube and Amazon to garner data regarding Coca Cola and people's opinions relating to Coca Cola. Besides web scraping of data, we also sieved through the beverage industry's annual reports to gain further insights of the competitive environment as a whole before performing text analytics using wordclouds and sentimental analysis on the data. All findings have been presented in a report.
